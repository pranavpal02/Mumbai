About Mumbai

Mumbai is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is the most populous city in India and the seventh most populous city in the world. Mumbai lies on the west coast of India and has a deep natural harbor. It is also the financial, commercial, and entertainment capital of India.
